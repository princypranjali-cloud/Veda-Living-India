Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At Veda Living India, we understand that data security is just as vital as physical wellness. This document outlines how we process data across our platform forms.

1. Information Gathered In Good Faith

We only collect personal information when you explicitly share it with us. This includes your Name, Email address, or Phone numbers provided through our Connect Form workspaces or during direct ordering via WhatsApp or Amazon tracking channels.

2. Data Storage & System Security

Your communication data is processed through highly secure cloud gateways (such as GitHub server nodes and Netlify protocols). We never lease, trade, or distribute your email records or profile databases to third-party commercial advertisement systems. All logs are monitored solely to fulfill product inquiries or share educational content updates when you subscribe to our lifestyle podcasts.

3. Transactional Safety Agreements

When redirecting to Amazon or completing secure API standard transaction interfaces, your confidential banking data remains fully encrypted under their respective certified security firewalls. Veda Living India never reads or stores your raw card pins or financial account data passwords.
